About the Artist

Aurelia Vance

For over a decade, Aurelia has collaborated with international publications, high-end fashion designers, and local galleries to portray visual statements. Inspired by minimalism, architectural shapes, and daylight shadows, every photo session is designed as a custom piece of art.

Utilizing medium format cameras, each shot is chosen intentionally, focusing on authentic expression, depth, and gold-hued natural illumination.
